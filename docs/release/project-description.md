# Happy Creeper

Short summary:

Turn creepers into real survival companions by taming them, managing their gear, recoloring them, and bringing them with you into combat and exploration.

## Overview

Happy Creeper is a NeoForge mod built around one idea: creepers should be more than walking explosions.

Instead of treating every creeper as a threat, you can work through a survival progression loop that lets you calm them down, weaken them, tame them, and keep them as persistent companions. The mod is designed to feel like a natural extension of survival gameplay rather than a creative-only pet system.

## Core Gameplay Loop

1. Wear a Creeper Head or Happy Creeper Mask to approach safely.
2. Feed a creeper an Anti-Blast Biscuit to weaken it.
3. Feed the weakened creeper a Sweet Gunpowder Biscuit to tame it.
4. Open the companion UI to manage its equipment and stored fuel.
5. Command it to follow or stay depending on the situation.
6. Keep it healthy, geared, and ready to fight alongside you.

## Companion Features

- Tamed creepers follow their owner and can be switched between follow and stay
- They teleport back when left behind and can move with their owner across dimensions
- Ownership and behavior remain stable after respawns and world travel
- They defend their owner and keep pressure on hostile mobs during fights
- Their attack pattern is adapted for companion gameplay instead of normal suicidal explosions
- They have stronger survival utility than normal creepers, including higher durability
- They can equip helmets and chestplates
- They can store fuel and use it to self-heal automatically
- They work with leads and fit into normal overworld travel better than vanilla creepers
- They no longer block sleeping like hostile nearby monsters

## Customization

- Tamed creepers use a custom happy creeper appearance by default
- They can be recolored with dyes
- Reusing the same dye does not waste the item
- Rainbow mode adds a smooth color-cycling effect
- The companion UI shows the creeper in 3D with its current state and gear

## Companion UI

- 3D creeper preview
- Helmet slot
- Chestplate slot
- Fuel slot
- Health display
- Active effect icons
- Follow/stay control
- Custom name display support

## Included Items

- Biscuit
- Anti-Blast Biscuit
- Sweet Gunpowder Biscuit
- Rainbow Biscuit
- Happy Creeper Mask

## Focus

Happy Creeper is aimed at players who want a more interactive creeper experience without discarding survival balance. The mod focuses on taming progression, practical companion behavior, visual identity, and small quality-of-life details that make the companion feel reliable in regular play.
